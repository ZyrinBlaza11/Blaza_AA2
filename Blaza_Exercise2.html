One Piece

As people set out on perilous expeditions in an effort to locate the One Piece and become the Pirate King. Monkey D. Luffy, a young boy, is one of them. One day, Luffy eats the mysterious Devil Fruit Gomu Gomu no Mi by accident, turning his body into rubber. Luffy makes the commitment to become the Pirate King after being motivated by both his metamorphosis and a pirate group called the Shanks. His only objective was to achieve complete freedom. Luffy assembles an eclectic group of companions for his adventure, including the swordsman Zoro, the navigator Nami, the sharpshooter Usopp, and the chef Sanji.
